County Tomato Shortage

Published, October 28th, 2006

    Everyone who knows, knows that the sauce makes the pizza...
    Unfortunately this month pizza sales have been booming and
    there is a PSA declaring tomatoes IN HIGH DEMAND.
    My original thought was "this can't be happening"...
    Don't get me wrong, I have no problem with truffle sauce,
    white sauce, or maybe pesto alternatives; I'm just not sure
    how long the city can make it without our beloved tomatoes.
    Chef's all across the city are planting their own tomatoes and
    starting to set up indoor hydroponic garden's in order to get
    back to making sauce asap.

Pizza Chef Falls Into Oven

Published, October 22nd, 2006

    Local pizza legend Alberto Russo was rushed to the hospital
    thursday night with numerous burn wounds. Apparently he
    was cooking one of his iconic calzones and slipped on an
    olive that was dropped at the entrance to the brick oven
    while reaching in to check on the calzone. Restaurant staff
    rushed to hoist Alberto out of the oven saving him from
    further injury. Food critique 'Giovani Gustav' commented
    saying that, "This should make other chefs more aware of the
    importance to maintaining an orderly kitchen".
