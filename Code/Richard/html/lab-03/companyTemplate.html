About Arcadia

Founded in 1823, Arcadia University is Arcadia's leading institution of higher learning. Our over 1,000 faculty members offer 73 degree programs to 15,000 students from more than 100 countries. Aradia Alumni serve in leading positions in business, government, academia, the arts, and the non-profit sector.

Aracadia's library system holds over one million volumes and includes state-of-the-art digital holdings. Arcadia was ranked among the world's top one hundred universities in 2018's Global University Assessment.
